Stemgarden. 2014

Initially a bamboo urban gardening solution project, Stemgarden is a project exchange platform that brings nature back to the city. The project will not lead to a commercial solution but will eventually be transformed into an online platform for sharing green projects for urban areas. The platform is still accessible online, www.stemgarden.co, but no longer updated.


The project was born during an entrepreneurial training course organised by Impulse Brussels. The initial idea was to propose an innovative hydroponic system, without electricity, built from a bamboo stick. The three-month training allowed us to mature the project on the financial, logistical and marketing aspects.
